Mission Planning UgCS

For this setup, the software UgCs is used. This software controls and operates uncrewed aerial vehicles (UAVs) remotely. UGCS includes hardware (computer - tablet) and software components, such as a control station, communication equipment, and navigation systems that allow an operator to remotely start, pause, stop, resume, and restart missions.
