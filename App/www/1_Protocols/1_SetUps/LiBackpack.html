Backpack DGC50

In this document, you will find all the steps to operate the DJI-M300 in combination with the Micasense Altum camera, from the mission planning to the processing of the data going through all to-dos before the flights and the tasks you must perform to ensure the success of your mission. When a word is highlighted, it contains an additional explanation when hovering the mouse on top of it; for example: term. Additionally, at the end of this document, you can download a field version that contains all the summarised steps, checklists and all the things you must remember for the equipment operation.

⚠ warning: You must read through all these documents before proceeding with the equipment you will use. It's your responsibility to have clearance on how to operate the equipment and how to act in case of emergencies. If any information provided is unclear or contradictory, please contact the UAS team in time to solve the uncertainties.

Assembly Checklist

  • Place the LiBackpack DGC50's storage control unit and scanner arm on the desktop.
    (?)
  • Plug the scanner arm into LiBackpack DGC50 storage and control unit. The tilted laser scanner should be opposite to the main body.
    (?)
  • You can hear a click sound when locking the scanner onto the main body. Then, tighten the screw to secure mounting.
    (?)
  • Loosen the two locking rings of the scanner rod counterclockwise, so the scanner rod can rise and fall freely.
    (?)
  • Try the telescopic rod and adjust the rod length using both hands.
    (?)
  • Secure the adjustment by screwing the locking rings tightly.
    (?)
  • Plug in the connection cable into the scanner arm and main body separately. Note: Align the port by the corresponding red dots.
    (?)
  • Assembly done.
    (?)
