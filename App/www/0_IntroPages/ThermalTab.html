Instructions for the Thermal Calibration

This Tab is designed to assist you in calibrating thermal images captured with DJI sensors using the official Thermal SDK (TSDK).

TSDK (DJI Thermal software development kit) is primarily used for the analysis and processing of infrared photos. Users can develop their own software based on TSDK in both Windows and Linux environments, enabling tasks such as R-JPEG infrared photo processing and temperature measurement.

Follow these steps to get started:

  • Step 1: Download the SDK by clicking the button below.
  • Step 2: Create your folder structure to keep all your information organized. A well-structured folder system helps you manage your data efficiently.
  • Step 3: Copy your photos to the designated folder. This process will copy and organize them within the new structure, ensuring everything is in its place.
  • Step 4: Perform the thermal calibration using the TSDK. This step is crucial for preparing your thermal images for analysis.
  • Step 5: After calibration, review some of the calibrated images to ensure they meet your quality standards and requirements.
